ABOUT US

Scott Walker has over 40 years experience in all aspects of the bicycle industry. Our specialties include vintage roads bikes and a wide variety of new bikes.

Our mission is to provide quick service at reasonable prices while also offering a wide variety of parts and accessories. We have the knowledge and experience to provide the best personal service possible.
